    Jahdai Searles (Multimedia Designer)

    Hello my name is Jahdai Searles, 21 years old and I am a Multimedia Designer. I had been designing and building things on media since 2009 when I was introduced to media arts and design in my highschool education. I love being part of a whole project which includes pre production, production and editing in post production. I love seeing project put together for the end result.

    I have a wide knowledge based around media which includes video editing, camera angles, camera lighting, photography, web design using html and css, photo editing using photoshop, and vector graphics and designs. I have a number of skills but my greatest strength would have to be video production and making elements look good on photoshop. My personality traits would have to be hard working individual and always tries my best in everything I do.
